## 📐 FRAMEWORK: HOW TO BUILD WINNING POSTS

### Phase 1: Research (30%)
1. Analyze top 3-5 ranking competitors
2. Identify THEIR gaps (not copied)
3. Find unanswered questions
4. Pick topic with UNIQUE angle

### Phase 2: Structure (20%)
1. H1: Optimized title with primary keyword
2. Meta description: 150-155 chars + CTA
3. H2 sections answering real questions
4. FAQ section with Schema markup

### Phase 3: Writing (30%)
1. Natural language (no keyword stuffing)
2. Cited data and statistics
3. Specific examples (not generic)
4. First-hand experience: "En nuestra experiencia..."
5. Long but scannable prose

### Phase 4: Technical SEO (10%)
1. Schema Article or FAQ
2. Internal linking to other DayByDay posts
3. External links to authorities
4. Optimized images with alt text
5. Clean URL (slug = primary keyword)

### Phase 5: Promotion (10%)
1. Share on LinkedIn
2. Newsletter (if audience exists)
3. Backlink outreach
4. Consistent publishing schedule
